About Me

My Background

My name is Katy Daviscourt and I grew up right here in Seattle, Washington. It's a beautiful place that I am happy to call home again after living for four years in California. I have been working as an American Sign Language (ASL) interpreter for the last several years, and while I will be sad to leave that community, I look forward to the opportunities ahead of me. I hope in the future to find a way to tie my knowledge of the Deaf community into my work as a developer. Some of my past achievements are:

  • Getting my Bachelor Degree in English from the University of Washington
  • Earning a second degree in Interpreting
  • Mastering a second language (ASL)
  • Owning a french bulldog
